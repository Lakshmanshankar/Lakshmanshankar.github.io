### Tools & Setup in 2025

#### **Daily Apps**

* **Browser**: Brave (Personal), Chrome (Work)
* **Terminal**: Kitty & Warp
* **Editor**: VSCode (Frontend), Neovim (Go, Python, TS)
* **Password Manager**: Bitwarden
* **Productivity**: Google Workspace (Mail, Calendar, Tasks)
* **Notes**: Notion & Excalidraw
* **OS**: Ubuntu 24.04 (Personal, with Nix store), macOS (Work)

I’m also a big fan of GNOME and its cohesive app ecosystem — it just feels incredibly usable and well thought out.

#### **Command Line Tools**

* **Shell**: zsh with Starship
* **jq + jnv**: Daily JSON processing
* **zoxide**: Smarter navigation
* **Neovim**: NvChad-based custom config

#### **Hardware**

* **Personal**: Acer Aspire (Ubuntu 24.04 + Nix store)

  * i5 12th Gen, 8GB RAM, 512GB SSD
* **Work**: MacBook Pro (M4)
* **Accessories**: Basic wireless keyboard & mouse
